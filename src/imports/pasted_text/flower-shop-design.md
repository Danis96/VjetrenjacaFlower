Flower Shop Website – Cvjećara Vjetrenjača

Design a beautiful, modern, delicate website for a flower shop in Sarajevo called “Cvjećara Vjetrenjača – Margareta”.

The brand should feel:

elegant

natural

delicate

premium

romantic

modern boutique style

This is not a generic e-commerce flower shop, but a premium local florist experience.

The design should evoke the feeling of fresh flowers, spring, weddings, gifts, and special moments.
